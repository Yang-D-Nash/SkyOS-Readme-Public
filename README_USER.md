## What SkyOS Is

SkyOS is the mobile product layer of the Skydown ecosystem. It combines AI, creator media,
membership, merch commerce, and support/legal paths inside one native iOS and Android experience.

## Where Users Should Start

- Home for orientation and entry points
- AI for assistant, visual, and agent workflows
- Music and Video for creator media
- Shop and Orders for merch and purchase visibility
- Settings for membership, legal, support, and account actions
