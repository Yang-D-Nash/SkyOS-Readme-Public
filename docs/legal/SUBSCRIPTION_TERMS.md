# SkyOS Subscription Terms

Status: repository operating version. Final subscription wording should be validated against the
active billing setup and target-market legal review before public launch.

## 1. Scope

SkyOS can offer recurring membership access for AI and related capability tiers. The exact plans,
names, pricing, and billing cadence depend on the currently active product configuration.

## 2. Pricing and Billing

- pricing should be shown before purchase
- the purchase flow should identify the active billing platform or hosted provider
- store-managed subscriptions follow the applicable App Store or Google Play billing rules
- hosted web subscription flows, where enabled, follow the active payment provider flow

## 3. Renewal and Cancellation

If a subscription renews automatically, that should be disclosed in the active purchase flow.
Cancellation and account-level management are generally handled through the store or provider that
owns the subscription transaction.

## 4. Restore

SkyOS should provide a restore path for existing entitlements where supported by the active billing
method. Restore visibility is part of product trust and should not be hidden.

## 5. Capability Model

SkyOS positions memberships as capability access, not as a token marketplace. Plans may differ by:

- AI quota
- workflow depth
- premium output access
- priority or support-related privileges

## 6. Support and Disputes

Billing support should use the active support route shown in the current release. Provider-specific
refund, cancellation, and dispute rules may depend on the store or payment platform used.

## 7. Final Review Required

Before public launch:

- confirm plan names and store metadata
- confirm restore behavior on live products
- confirm renewal and cancellation wording
- confirm legal review for the target markets
