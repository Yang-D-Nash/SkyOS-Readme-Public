# 22kydwn App Store Listing

This document defines the App Store presence for the 22kydwn product experience by Skydown.
It follows the naming lock in `docs/store-listing.md`.

## Positioning

Use one clear sentence everywhere:

> 22kydwn unifies AI, productivity, ZweiZwei music, Skydown Entertainment video, and established merch lines in one calm native app.

### Full Description

22kydwn is the umbrella app from Skydown — one calm native home for AI, productivity, media, and commerce.

Under 22kydwn, sub-brands stay distinct: **ZweiZwei** for music, **Skydown Entertainment** for video, and established merch lines (**Skydown**, **ZweiZwei**, **Skydown x 22**) in the shop. Users move faster without switching between disconnected apps.

Reminder workflows are already end-to-end live: users can create a timed reminder in natural
language and receive a push notification on signed-in iPhone or Android devices when the reminder is
due.

AI and Agent are built as focused workspaces for prompts, responses, and guided execution. Music is
curated under **ZweiZwei**. Video is presented under **Skydown Entertainment**. Merch keeps its
existing line identities with a guided path from discovery to cart.

Tasks and Notes are live for structured capture, storage, and management in the app, and can also be
created from the Activepieces workflow API.

22kydwn provides visual consistency, motion language, and reliable interaction patterns across
supported platforms.

The app also includes account, settings, support, and legal surfaces to keep trust visible inside
the product.

## App Store Rules

- Keep the App Store product name as `22kydwn`.
- Use `Skydown` for operator/support/legal context, not as a competing app name in metadata.
- Name **Skydown Entertainment** in video copy and **ZweiZwei** in music copy.
- Do not use `SkyOS` in public metadata.
- Do not explain internal providers in primary public copy unless the user truly needs that detail.
- Mention payment or membership with clarity, never with urgency pressure.
- If a feature is gated by plan or rollout state, describe it honestly.
