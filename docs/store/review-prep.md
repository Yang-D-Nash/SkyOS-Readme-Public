## Review Request Timing

Ask for ratings after a clear success moment, not after friction.

Recommended moments:

- after a successful AI session that ends with a useful result
- after a successful agent outcome or clearly completed plan
- after a successful merch or order milestone when the user is satisfied
- after restore succeeds and the expected plan is visible again

Avoid asking:

- on first launch
- directly after login or registration
- right after an upgrade prompt
- after quota warnings or blocked states
- after a failed purchase, failed restore, or failed network action
- while the user is in support, settings, or legal recovery paths

## Subscription Clarity

Before public release, confirm that store copy and in-app copy agree on:

- plan names: `Free`, `Pro`, `Creator`
- capability-first positioning instead of token-shop language
- where restore is available
- where subscription management is available
- what paid value expands: reach, limits, priority, and stronger output depth

## Public Store Trust Checklist

- support email is visible and tested
- restore purchases is visible in the relevant membership surfaces
- legal links match the current operator details
- store metadata matches the shipped UI language
- screenshots do not expose owner-only or internal controls
- upgrade claims do not exceed what the live plan setup really unlocks

## Release Note

If billing, restore, support, or legal content changed in a release, store metadata and support
copy should be reviewed in the same release pass. Trust breaks fastest when the store promises one
thing and the app shows another.
