# 22kydwn Google Play Listing

This document defines the Google Play presence for the 22kydwn Android product experience by
Skydown. It follows the naming lock in `docs/store-listing.md`.

## Positioning

Use one sentence that explains the product immediately:

> 22kydwn unifies AI, productivity, ZweiZwei music, Skydown Entertainment video, and established merch lines on Android.

## Brand Roles

| Name | Public role |
| --- | --- |
| `22kydwn` | Google Play title and umbrella app experience |
| `Skydown` | Operator, support, legal, creator ecosystem |
| `Skydown Entertainment` | Video surfaces only |
| `ZweiZwei` | Music surfaces only |
| `Skydown x 22` (+ line marks) | Merch and commerce only — existing artwork |

## Recommended Metadata

### English

- Title: `22kydwn`
- Short Description:

  `AI, Music, Video and Merch in one calm premium app.`

### Long Description

22kydwn is the umbrella Android app from Skydown — AI, reminders, tasks, notes, media, and commerce in one premium structure.

Instead of fragmented tools, 22kydwn unifies the experience while keeping sub-brands readable: **ZweiZwei** for music, **Skydown Entertainment** for video, and established merch lines in the shop.

Reminder is already operational end to end: users can create reminders in natural language and get
push notifications on signed-in devices when reminders are due.

AI and Agent are designed as practical workspaces for input, context, and results. Music is
presented under **ZweiZwei**. Video is presented under **Skydown Entertainment**. Merch keeps its
existing line artwork with a guided commerce flow from product view to cart.

Tasks and Notes are live for structured capture, storage, and management in the app, with
Activepieces creation available through the workflow API.

22kydwn brings the product together through design language, visual hierarchy, motion rhythm, and
cross-platform continuity.

The app includes settings, support, and legal access points so users can understand account and
policy context directly inside the app.

## Google Play Rules

- Keep the Google Play product name as `22kydwn`.
- Use `Skydown` for operator/support/legal context, not as a competing store title.
- Name **Skydown Entertainment** in video copy and **ZweiZwei** in music copy.
- Do not use `SkyOS` in public metadata.
- Keep SEO natural; do not stuff repeated terms into the title or opening lines.
- The first two lines must explain user value, not internal infrastructure.
- Mention Android quality and large-screen readiness only when screenshots support it.
- Keep subscription language aligned with the in-app wording: capability-first, clear restore, clear management.
